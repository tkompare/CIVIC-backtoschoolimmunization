More Information

Immunization events are free for ages 6 months to 18 years of age. No appointment is needed. Please bring your child's shot record with you. For the safety of your child it is important for the immunization nurse to know what shots your child has already received and when they were given.

About the Author

Tom Kompare learned about this program while emailing with a person who works at Chicago Department of Public Health. He thought he should make a web application to help parents get their children immunized.

Tom believes in sharing. The code behind this site is freely available, under The MIT license, to anyone who may want to implement this in their city. You may find the code here. If you or your city needs help getting it up and running, feel free to contact Tom.
